BMI Calculator

With the metric system, the formula for BMI is weight in kilograms (kg) divided by height in meters (m) squared. Since height is commonly measured in centimeters (cm).

Calculate your BMI

The BMI is calculated by dividing an adult's weight in kilograms by their height in metres squared. For example, if you weigh 70kg and are 1.70m tall, you work out your BMI by: squaring your height in metres: 1.70 x 1.70 = 2.89.
